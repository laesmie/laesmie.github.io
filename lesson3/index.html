Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is for anyone to come to this page and check on the wheather, no matter where they are, or when they want to check. Weather is one of the biggest reasons that events and vacations are canceled or post-poned. I hope to be able to help those that are planning such things in their lives to help better prepare for the day!
