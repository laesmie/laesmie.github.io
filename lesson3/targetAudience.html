Target Audience

The target audience for this website is anyone that is needing to check on the weather, either in their area or in an area that they are wanting to travel to. Weather is a very unpredictable force. So, it is always nice to be able to know what to expect.

Personas

  • Kaylee: 27-year-old spelunker. She loves to go out and hike and be outdoors. Knowing what the weather is going to be like for the day is very important to her. Knowing what the weather will be like helps her to be able to dress and pack for each new adventure.
  • Kevin: 42-year-old writer who needs to know what the weather is like during certain times of the year to help make his stories more believeable. Being able to come to this site and go to a certain day to know what the weather was/will be is very important and helpful for him.

Scenarios

  • A visitor can come to this site to check on what the weather in their area will be like for that day so they know how they need to dress.
  • Someone planning a vacation to an area they have never been to before can come to this site to see what the weather will be like. This will allow them to better plan activies, and pack accordingly.
  • While writing a new story, writers would be able to visit this site to be able to check on the weather in the area they are writing about. This helps to give a more realistic tone to the story.
