Target Audience

The target audience for this website is anyone that is planning a trip to the Cozumel region (either in Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock in el Centro de Cozumel or near the Terminal Puerta Maya), that are in need of some kind of transportation. The site offers an array of choices from scooters, to ATV's, to Jeeps.

Personas

  • Michelle: 51-year-old family enthusiast. She loves planning family vacations, but she hates how much it costs to get transportation for her massive family of 7.
  • Tyler: 20-year-old traveler. He wanted to save up enough money to be able to travel for a year before starting college. They find themselves in Mexico near the end of their journey and needs to get some kind of transportation to see more of the city before heading home to start school.

Scenarios

  • Someone planning a vacation to Cozumel region needs to rent a type of vehicle to get around the town while there.
  • While on a stop in Cozumel while on a cruise, they are in search of a vehicle to get them around the city as quickly as possible before having to return to the ship.
